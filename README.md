# BigFatPrompts 🍔

**Big, fat prompts for building in [Framer](https://www.framer.com/agents/).**

A curated, open directory of copy-paste prompts for the Framer agent — landing pages, animations, CMS, copywriting, SEO and more. Find a good one, copy it, paste it into the Framer agent, ship a better site.

🔗 **Live:** [bigfatprompts.com](https://bigfatprompts.com)

Built for the **Framer Agents Hackathon (2026)**.

---

## What is this?

Think [cursor.directory](https://cursor.directory), but for prompting the Framer agent. Every prompt is:

- **Copy-paste ready** — written the way you'd actually talk to the agent.
- **Specific** — encodes real design, UX and copywriting best practices.
- **Customisable** — `[bracketed]` placeholders mark what to swap for your project.
- **Categorised & searchable** — filter by category, search by keyword, sort by top-rated.

## Categories

Landing Pages & Heroes · Layout & Structure · Animation & Effects · CMS & Dynamic Content · Copywriting & Messaging · SEO & Performance · Forms & Conversion · Responsive & Mobile · Design Systems & Theming · Interactions & Scroll

## Contribute a prompt

Got a prompt that consistently produces great results in Framer? **[Open an issue](https://github.com/utrpaul/bigfatprompts/issues/new)** with:

- **Title** — short and punchy
- **Category** — one of the above
- **Prompt** — the full text, with `[placeholders]`
- **Who it's for** — one line

Good prompts get merged into the directory.

## Tech

Plain, fast, dependency-free static site (HTML/CSS/JS). Deployed on Vercel. Ratings are stored locally in your browser.
